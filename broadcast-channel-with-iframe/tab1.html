When you click the button, the following will happen:

  • This tab (Tab 1), of origin X, will open a new tab (Tab 2) of origin Y
  • Tab 2 will redirect to Tab 3 of origin Z
  • Tab 3 will message the iframe (of origin Z) in Tab 1 using `BroadcastChannel`
  • The iframe will pass the message to Tab 1 via the iframe method of `parent.postMessage`
  • Tab 1 will receive the message, and close the tab it opened
